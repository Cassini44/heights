Adult Abbreviated Program

In Ohio, individuals aged 18 years or older who fail their first attempt at the driving test must complete an abbreviated adult driver training course before they can retake the test. This includes:

  1. State approved course
    • 4 hours in person
      • OR
    • 4 hours online
  2. Driving
    • 4 hours with a licensed instructor with a licensed abbreviated adult driver training school
      • OR
    • 24 hours with a licensed driver that is 21 or older with a completed affidavit
